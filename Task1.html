New GST rates take effect today

New GST rates 2025 full list effective today

In a big cheer at the start of the festival season the next generation GST reforms announced by the molic government give into effect today on September 22nd 2025.
From today, a plethora of household items cars, TVs, bikes, will become cheaper for the common man and Middle Class. Many food items have been removed from the NIL or 0% tax bracket these includes Roti, paratha, paneer, Khakra extra importantly health and life insurance will be exempt from GST.
The new GST rates first announced by FM Nirmala sitaraman earlier this month reduce the text lapse to 2 board rates 5% and 18% while most items under GST have seen great quotes some Limited items will now also be there with higher tax rates such as clothes price above rupees 2500 will now attract a higher GST rate of 18% compared to 12% before.
